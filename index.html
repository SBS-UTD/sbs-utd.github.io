Welcome to the official page of Sommiloni of Bengali Students. We are a vibrant student organization at the University of Texas at Dallas, representing the Bengali community from South East Asia.

Our mission is to foster community support, celebrate our rich culture and traditions, and share our heritage with the diverse UTD community in the most authentic way.
